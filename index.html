    What are the best ingredients for a weight loss smoothie?

    You might have heard that smoothies are a great way to lose weight. They're also an easy way to get more fruits and vegetables in your diet. But if you're trying to lose weight, you want to make sure that your smoothie isn't packed with calories.

    Fruits and vegetables: These are loaded with fiber and vitamins but low in calories. Aim for at least one serving of fruit or vegetable per drink.

    Nonfat milk or yogurt: Milk adds protein and calcium without adding too many extra calories. Yogurt is also high in protein and calcium — plus probiotics, which help keep digestion regular and healthy bacteria levels up in your gut!
